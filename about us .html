About Us

Sri Lanka's National Carrier, an award-winning airline that was founded in 1979, has a strong reputation for customer service, comfort, safety, dependability, and punctuality.The airport in Colombo, Bandaranaike International Airport, serves as the airline's hub and offers quick access to its extensive worldwide route network of 126 destinations in 61 countries.

We are here to give good,reliable and efficient service for all the customers who entrust us and get our service.

Our mission to give the best to our customers. We are dedicated to do our duty within 24 hours throughput the year. From 1997 we are given a very satisfactory service to the customer all over the country. So, customers have predicated us as a very trustworthy company.
